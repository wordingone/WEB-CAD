# CAD Blocks Library — Source Provenance

Generated for #1853. Each block is an original CC0 SVG implementation of a standardized architectural drafting convention. No code or artwork was copied from any external source.

## Source standards cited

| Standard | Full title |
|---|---|
| AIA | AIA Architectural Graphic Standards, 11th ed. (Wiley, 2007). Chapter 3: Symbols and Conventions. |
| ANSI Y14.2 | ANSI Y14.2-2014: Line Conventions and Lettering for Engineering Drawings. |
| ISO 7519 | ISO 7519:1991: Technical drawings — General principles of presentation for general arrangement and assembly drawings. |

Architectural plan symbols (door swings, window lines, plumbing fixture outlines, furniture outlines, entourage circles, vegetation circles) are **functional/utilitarian graphic conventions** defined by the above standards. They are not copyrightable as creative works under 17 U.S.C. § 102(b) (useful article doctrine). Our SVG implementations are original artwork and are dedicated to the public domain under **CC0 1.0 Universal**.
